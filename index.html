Join our community.
We're constantly changing for the better as we listen to the feedback of our community. Here's all we have to offer!
Technology

We're constantly looking to improve our VTC. We've introduced our Drivers Hub which all of our drivers use to log jobs and compete with each other through a leaderboard.

Ranking

Our internal ranking system means that for every kilometre you drive, you earn one point. Points can be multiplied for TruckersMP events, and internal events. Your goal is to become the highest rank!

Support

Our support system ensures you're never alone on your journey, offering guidance, resources, and solutions when you need them.

Community

Even though our community is small, we're growing fast and we want you to be a part of our journey. Join Presto today!

Experience our Drivers Hub

Our Drivers Hub is at the core of our VTC! It is constantly being developed to bring new features that our community will love! Our Drivers Hub has so many features, including:

Job Tracking

Track your jobs seamlessly with our Job Tracking software! All you need to do is download the application, and drive to your destination!

Leaderboards

Compete with our drivers by getting the most amount of points! Our best performing driver will be awarded with the Driver of the Week badge!

Events System

Our events system syncs with TruckersMP and also allows for internal Presto events! We're constantly hosting events, so keep an eye out!
